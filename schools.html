Value Study for Schools

Value Study helps students understand light, shadow, and composition with a classroom-friendly toolkit that works on iOS, macOS, and Android. This page outlines how to bring Value Study to your school and request enrollment through Apple School Manager.

Email us about school access

Education offering

Schools receive guided onboarding, privacy-minded configuration, and support for art classes exploring tonal values. The app keeps student data on-device and uses optional, privacy-focused analytics to improve the experience without collecting personal information.

Value Study is free to download on every platform, making it simple to deploy across mixed device environments.

Apple School Manager enrollment

  1. Open Apple School Manager and sign in with your administrator account.
  2. Search the App Store section for “Value Study” (bundle ID: id1546616127).
  3. Choose your deployment method: Apps and Books (recommended), device assignments, or managed distribution.
  4. Assign licenses to classes or devices and push Value Study via your MDM.
  5. Share the quick-start guide with teachers: download the app, load a photo, and use the value picker to highlight tonal ranges.
